-who we are-

We are a GeoData Science Consulting company that uses artificial intelligence (AI) in solving geospatial related challeges. AI is an advanced analytical tool that has facilitated us to do spatial modelling of complex, heterogeneous and uncertain Geodata. By modelling frameworks, we convert the spatial data into usable information and knowledge.
